How to Prepare Jollof Rice

Step 1
Cut,rinse, boil and season the chicken, then fry it
Step 2
Blend pepper,tomatoes and onion together.
Step 3
Fry the mixture in oil and ass seasonig,curry and tyhme
Step 4
Add chicken stock and allow to boil
Step 5
Add rice and cook until soft and dry
Step 6
